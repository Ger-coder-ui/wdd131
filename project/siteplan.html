Skate Pulse – Website Plan

Site Name

Skate Pulse

I chose the name Skate Pulse because it represents the energy, movement, and rhythm of the skating lifestyle. My community currently has no centralized resource for skate gear, events, or updates. This name captures both the culture and the purpose of the website.

Optional domain availability: skatepulse-shop.com (not required for class)

Site Purpose

Skate Pulse will serve as a central hub for the local skating community. It will show available skate shop items, highlight promotions, announce new stock, and provide updated information on upcoming skating events. The site exists to fill a major gap—currently, locals often hear about events only a few days before they happen, and it’s difficult to find reliable information about skate equipment.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find the latest skateboards or gear available in the shop?
  • When and where is the next skating event in the community?

Color Schema

Electric Blue (#0d6efd) – Used for headings, borders, and primary accents.

Skate Orange (#ff4500) – Used for buttons, highlights, and calls-to-action.

Typography

Impact – Used for headings to reflect a bold, street-style skate aesthetic.
Arial – Used for body text for readability and simplicity.
